UEFA Champions League

The most coveted competition in club football today, the Champions League has been around for 67 years. Formerly called the European Cup, the tournament was the stage for several superstars to put their names in football history. But who are the players who have won the most famous Champions League?

Top 3 Champions

One of the best football clubs in the world, Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, ​​is the first of the greatest champions of the Champions League for its 14 titles. In 2004 Real Madrid received the FIFA Order of Merit, the organization's highest honor normally given to people, for having contributed significantly to football.

Associazione Calcio Milan is seven times champion of the UEFA Champions League. being 4 times runner-up in 1957–58, 1992–93, 1994–95, and 2004–05. Milan is the Italian club with the most international titles.

Fußball-Club Bayern München is 5 times champions of the European Champions League and has been runners-up 5 times. In 2013 Bayern Munich became the first German football team to win the treble and the seventh in Europe.

the elite champions

Cristiano Ronaldo

The five-time champion, CR7 is the only athlete at the top of the list to have won the competition for more than one club. The guy is also the top scorer in the Champions League of all time. With all his determination, it would not be strange to see him seeking first place in this ranking. Never doubt Christian.

Paolo Maldini

The Italian is also a record holder when it comes to the number of Champions League finals. With eight finals under his belt, he is a record holder in this field. With the first of the finals being played in 1989 and the last one in 2007, the achievements are the result of a career marked by longevity and regularity.

Karim Benzema

At 33 years old, Karim Benzema is still at his peak. The forward finished as the 2nd top scorer in La Liga, behind only Leo Messi last season. Benzema still has one more season left on his current contract with Real Madrid and has publicly declared his desire to renew it and end his career at Real Madrid.

PSG managed a wide victory in the first match, 4-0. However, in the second match, Barcelona still believed in the classification. Milan 3 x 3 L.F.C

The famous “Istanbul miracle” could not be left out of our list of the 10 greatest Champions League games. In the first half alone, Milan managed to take the advantage of three goals. But in the second half, Gerrard, Šmicer, and Xabi Alonso managed to tie the game. And, on penalties, goalkeeper Jerzy Dudek was the great hero of the match and secured the title for Liverpool.

RMD 4 x1 A Madrid

And here we go for another match that went down in history in the last few minutes. Until the 43rd minute of the second half, Atlético de Madrid had the 2013/14 cup under their arms. But on the other side was Real Madrid and, at the end of the second half, Ramos tied the game and took the game to extra time.
